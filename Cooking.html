About us

Our story

Welcome to Cooking with Tayfun and Joao, where culinary passion meets creative flair. Our journey in the world of cooking began with a shared love for the art of gastronomy and a desire to explore the diverse flavors and techniques from around the globe.

Tayfun, born and raised in the enchanting city of Istanbul, developed his culinary skills amidst the vibrant tapestry of Turkish cuisine. He fondly recalls his childhood spent in his grandmother's kitchen, where the secrets of age-old recipes were passed down through generations. His culinary adventure took flight as he ventured beyond the boundaries of his homeland, discovering the magic of Mediterranean and Middle Eastern flavors.

Joao's culinary odyssey started amidst the picturesque landscapes of Portugal. Growing up near the azure shores of the Atlantic Ocean, he developed an affinity for the freshest seafood and the vibrant, sun-kissed produce of the region. His culinary journey was further enriched through his travels across Europe, where he unearthed a treasure trove of gastronomic traditions.







Who We Are

Tayfun and Joao are not just two ordinary cooks; we are culinary enthusiasts on a mission to inspire your inner chef. Our backgrounds and experiences bring a unique blend of traditions and innovation to the kitchen.

Tayfun's expertise lies in the intricate art of Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisine. He has a knack for infusing aromatic spices and vibrant ingredients into every dish, creating an explosion of flavors that tantalize the taste buds.

Joao, on the other hand, adds a touch of coastal charm to our culinary endeavors, making seafood dishes and grilled delicacies his specialty.
